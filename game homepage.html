Biodegradable

Waste breaks down naturally and can be used for composting.

Recyclable

Items that can be processed and reused again.

Non-Biodegradable

Materials that don't decompose easily and harm the environment.

Hazardous

Waste that poses a threat to health or environment.

EcoSort Challenge

Learn to sort, protect the Earth.
